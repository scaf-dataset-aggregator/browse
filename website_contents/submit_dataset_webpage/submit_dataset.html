What counts as "relevant enough"?

It should be of relevance to the SCAF network. Generally, this means that the dataset aligns with the goals of the SCAF research community, supports ongoing studies, or provides useful insights for related projects.

Why should I upload?

Visibility on Search engines

Your dataset might be publicly accessible online, but not visible in search results. Our pages implement the Dataset schema (guide), as defined here: https://schema.org/Dataset. Once added, your dataset will be discoverable by Google.

Visibility to the SCAF network

Your dataset will stand out within the SCAF research network, making it easier for researchers to find and use relevant data.
