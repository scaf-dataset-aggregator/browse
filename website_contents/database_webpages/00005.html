National Diet and Nutrition Survey (Scotland)

Keywords: scotland, nutrient intakes, nutritional status, adult, children

Shareability: Publicly shareable

Status: Complete

Data types: Numeric / Quantitative (.csv, .xlsx, .tsv, .ods), xlsx, csv

Abstract

Dataset produced by the UK/Scottish government. Food consumption, nutrient intakes and nutritional status of the Scottish population - adults and children from 18 months upwards. Results are presented by age/sex sub-groups.

Long description

The original dataset was produced by the UK Government, through the Food Standards Agency (FSA).

Although the dataset is supposed to be available on the UK Open Data website, this source does not appear to have been maintained. Instead, please consult the first link to the Food Standards Agency website.

Format and Contents

  • Format: RFDA
  • What to look for: Entries labelled “Excel”

The files contain descriptive statistics for many nutrients and other chemicals, including:

  • Mean
  • Median
  • Standard deviation (SD)
  • 2.5th percentile
  • 97.5th percentile

Data Structure

  • Data are separated by gender and age group:

  • 11–18

  • 19–64
  • 65+

There are also several reference tables explaining the meaning of each term used in the dataset.
