Scottish Agricultural Census

Keywords: land use, farm, employees, occupiers, ownership, legal responsibility, emissions, nitrogen, manure, soil testing, fertiliser, livestock, cattle, sheet, pigs, poultry, crops, cereals, oilseed, potatoes, beans, stock feeding, vegetables, fruits, machinery, fuel, income

Shareability: Publicly shareable

Status: Continuously updated

Data types: Numeric / Quantitative, Maps / Spatial, xlsx, ods

Abstract

A census and report carried by the Scottish Government on farms. It contains estimates relating to almost all aspects relating to farms, from Land Use, Ownership, emissions, soil, livestock, crops, machinery and income. The data can be found on the Scottish Government website for every year.

Long description

Provides Scotland totals from the published results of the annual June Scottish Agricultural Census.

The latest edition of this publication is listed below and incorporates all revisions applied up to the date of publication. Details of historic revisions applicable to this and other agricultural statistics publications can be found at revisions to agricultural statistics publications.

Records going back to 1999 may be accessed at our online archive.
