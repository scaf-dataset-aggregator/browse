Food Standards Scotland - Animal Welfare Data

Keywords: enforcement, inspection, food, standards, scotland, cattle, welfare

Shareability: Publicly shareable

Status: Continuously updated

Data types: Textual, xlsx

Abstract

This dataset provided by the FSS includes various reports relating to violations of Animal Welfare regulations, specifically those which scores 3 or 4. It records the species, the "violation score", the type of non-compliance, detailed descriptions and the actions taken. It even includes *anonymised* emails from the offending parties. The spreadsheets are also split by where the accident took place: "on farm & transport" vs "In plant".

Long description

This dataset is made available by FSS thanks to the Freedom of Information Act. The dataset is a listing of various welfare violations relating to farm animals, with each month being its own spreadsheet. Each spreadsheet has 2 sheets: On Farm & Transport, and In Plant.

Each sheet contains the following columns:

  • Date of incident

  • Species:

    • Poultry

    • Sheep

    • Pigs

    • Cattle

  • Score: either 3 or 4

    • 3 – Potential Risk to animal welfare with no evidence of pain and suffering

    • 4 – Imminent Risk to animal welfare and evidence of pain and suffering

  • Non-compliance group

    • On farm welfare

    • Welfare on transport

    • Captive bold / concussion …

    • etc..

  • Non-compliance type: more detail on the non-compliance group

  • Non-compliance: even more detail on the non-compliance

  • Description: the text report written by the inspector, anonymised

  • Action: the action taken to resolve the situation

  • Date feedback received: when the complaint was acknowledged by the offending party

  • Feedback text: Format of reply, usually “email”

  • Feedback: the reply from the farm / plant, usually with plenty of detail.
