Food Hygiene Information Scheme (Food Standards Scotland)

Keywords: food, hygiene, rating, inspection, restaurants, fss, enforcement

Shareability: Publicly shareable

Status: Continuously updated

Data types: Numeric / Quantitative, Markup, xml

Abstract

Food hygiene rating or inspection results given to a business and date of inspection or visit by the local authority. Businesses include: restaurants, pubs, cafés, takeaways, hotels and other places consumers eat, as well as in supermarkets and other food shops. The data is held on behalf of Scottish local authorities participating in the FSS' national food hygiene information scheme

Long description

Food Standards Scotland carries out various inspections and ratings for businesses. The data provided is in XML, and contains the detail of each business. The main field is "RatingValue", which can have values:

  • Pass

  • Improvement Required

  • Awaiting Inspection

  • Exempt

Note that each XML file is in a single line, and is not in human-readable format.

The data is available for every county in Scotland

Methodology

Inspectors for every area follow this manual: https://bryantresearch.co.uk/foodlaw/pdf/uk-11010-training-manual-scotland.pdf
