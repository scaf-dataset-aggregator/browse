Multi-Species Ante Mortem & Post Mortem Conditions (2016-2018)

Keywords: data, inspection, monitoring, processing, conditions, ante-mortem, post-mortem, disease, cattle, poultry, pig, goat, sheep

Shareability: Publicly shareable

Status: Complete

Data types: Numeric / Quantitative, Textual, xlsx

Abstract

Data relating to diseases found in animals being processed for food, both before and after death. Data collected by Food Standards Scotland staff at approved Scottish Meat establishments. Each species is reported on an individual tab and reported by month.

Long description

The dataset contains information for sheep, goat, poultry, pig. For each animal kind, the diseases are recorded with the following columns:

  • Date

  • Species

  • Ante-mortem conditions (if any) (text)

  • Number of conditions identified (how many times that condition was identified on that day)

  • Number rejected at Ante-mortem

  • Rejection Type

  • Type (of condition)

  • Post-mortem conditions identified (if any)

  • Number of conditions (of post-mortem)

Methodology

Methodology was not provided, it appears to be reported by processors.
